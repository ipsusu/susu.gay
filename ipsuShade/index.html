ipsuShade

is a ReShade preset collection (with GShade support) that aims to deliver maximum quality at maximum FPS with the Gameplay, Questing and Lite variant presets, but also provide high fidelity screenshots with the Screenie, Ultimate and Pro Suite preset types.

Each preset type comes in a range of 7 included colour variants.
